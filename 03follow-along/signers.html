Who Were the Signers

Most Famous Signers

John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress at the time; famous for his large and flamboyant signature on the Declaration.
Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration of Independence; later became the 3rd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned statesman, inventor, and diplomat; helped secure French support during the American Revolution.
John Adams
Leading advocate for independence; later became the 2nd President of the United States.
Samuel Adams
Organizer of the Boston Tea Party and influential revolutionary leader.
