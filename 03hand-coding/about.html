About

This website is a place where people can easliy find the best food to eat on the North Shore. Here we will put the location, reviews and menus so it is easier for you to decide what to eat on the North Shore. We only put the best eats here and make sure that it is easy to use.

Places to Eat

  1. Ken's Fish
  2. Seven Brothers
  3. Kahuku Farms

Ken's Fresh Fish (Laie)

  1. Beloved seafood spot known for fresh, made-to-order dishes.
  2. Menu changes daily based on the catch, ensuring freshness.
  3. Popular items include Ahi Katsu, spicy poke, and tempura shrimp.
  4. Generous portions and friendly service are appreciated by visitors.
  5. Some note that the seasoning can be minimal, and limited seating may require a wait during peak hours.

Seven Brothers (Kahuku)

  1. Offers a variety of burgers, including the Shem Burger and the Love Bowl.
  2. Food quality is praised, especially the coconut shrimp and seasoned fries.
  3. Casual walk-up service with picnic table seating creates a relaxed atmosphere.
  4. Occasional inconsistencies in food preparation, but overall experience is positive.
